About Me

Background

I'm originally from Chicago, where I grew up surrounded with art and design opportunities, granting me a gateway to kickstarting my professional career at TCU. Throughout my time at TCU, I've had the opportunity to lead within different communities and find areas where I thrive creatively. I served as merchandise design chair for my sorority chapter for two years, then leading me to the opportunity of holding the marketing director position on our executive board. Simultaneously, I've held a graphic design internship with a local events design company for two years, and have served in two different creative roles with TCU's student advertising and PR agency, Roxo. For the past two summers, I have served as a marketing intern with a software company in the music industry, located in Chicago. The software, Master Tour by Eventric, serves as a digital tourbook and touring software built for tour managers and artists. This experience has combined my digital marketing skills along with data analytics points that I have learned in the classroom. I've been so grateful for these opportunities thus far, and cannot wait to continue to grow my network and experiences, professionally. As I begin my final year of undergraduate studies, I have goals in expanding my current network, both in and around the music and live events industry. I am mostly interested in pursuing a career in the marketing communications and digital marketing realm, and look forward to the opportunity with presenting my refined skill set and valued education to my future employers.
